ABOUT US

We are a website that strives to build, provide and maintain an up-to-date menu database for restaurants, bars and whatnot throught the Costa del Sol.

We strive to provide a service for those buisnesses that are too busy to, cannot afford to, do not have the know-how or otherwise do not put their menu online themselves.

Due to this website being but a start-up stage, it still has some bugs and not all web pages have been built yet.

As our team collects raw data for this website it shall grow.

We also have plans in the future to:

(1) Include a map service on our website with geotagged places for which we provide menu's

(2) Build and release an app - which contains downloadable content, including maps and GPS locations

(3) Provide a translation option for client menu's

We offer peer reviews to help potential clientel choose dishes and so on.

We do not use cookie's nor trackers as we are advocates for user privacy.

We hope you enjoy our service :)


Do you have any queeries? Would you like us to put your menu online?

Then please contact us at kasgyfi@skdfyg.com
